Pixel Craft Season #6

Wiretap & Microphone

This is what is required for the Wiretap & Microphone recipe.

All you need to do is make a Calibrated Sculk Sensor and have 1 level to rename it in an Anvil to "wiretap" (this is not case-sensitive) and then take the microphone out and you will recieve the speaker in your inventory.
It is recommended that you have at lest 2 empty inventory slots when crafting this.
To use these just place the Microphone down where you want to listen in on and the speaker where you want to listen from, the 2 ends both have a radius of 32 blocks and once placed are always live.
