Radio

This is what is required for the Radio recipe.

To make a custom radio all you need to do is run the above command but replace the blue text with what ever MP3 Stream URL you want, and then replace the yellow text with what you want to call this station in game.
To activate the radio you place in down and right click on it, give it a second to fetch the stream and boom you can listen to the radio while within a 48 block radius.
